Some moves have secondary effects. These happen pretty infrequently, though for this demonstration they will always occur.

Thunderbolt has a 10% chance to paralyze its enemy.

Flamethrower has a 10% chance to burn its enemy.

Sludge Bomb has a 10% chance to poison its enemy.

Freeze Dry has a 10% chance to freeze its enemy.

Iron Head has a 30% chance of causing its opponent to flinch if this attack happens before the enemy attacks, making the enemy skip its turn.

Earth Power has a 10% chance of lowering the Special Defense of its target.
