Abilities are critically important in battles. Here is a demonstration of 6 abilities and how they assist in battle.

Levitate protects the Rotom-Wash from Ground-Type moves like Earth Power

Gholdengo's ability "Good as Gold" stops it from receiving negative status conditions like paralysis

Heatran's flash fire ability not only makes it immune to fire type moves but buffs its own fire type moves when the ability activates

Gargancal's ability Sturdy makes it immune to being knocked out from full HP in one shot.

Volcanion's ability Water Absorb makes it immune to water type moves.

Krookodile's ability Intimidate lowers its opponent's attack whenever it enters the battle, allowing it to survive a normally fatal attack
